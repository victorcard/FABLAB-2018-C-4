DIGITAL MANUFACTURING

EQUIPMENT CONFIGURATION

The configuration of the Laser Cutter, you must select RGB as print color and define the following requirements:

  • Material type: MDF
  • Thickness: 3 mm
  • Speed: 0.5
  • Power: 100%
  • PPI: 1250


    • CLICK HERE to download the Laser cutter configuration.

    SOFTWARE CONFIGURATION


    The design of five layers, each one with a design, these are placed one after the other at a distance of 25 mm which gives the sensation of depth.

    PRINTING

  • We wait five minutes for the refrigeration system to charge.
  • After waiting, we turn on the laser cutting machine.
  • Remove all elements and lower the platform to place the MDF.
  • Place the MDF and press Autofocus

    • ASSEMBLED


    For this last stage, all the pieces that do not correspond to the design are removed and are assembled or presented to ensure that all the pieces fit together.
